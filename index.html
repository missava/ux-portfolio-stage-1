about me

"Data-driven engineer with a UX designer's heart."

I am an Industrial Engineer that loves blending art and science. For most of my career, user experience has been part of my life as industrial engineering shares many parallels with user-centered design. Solving complex problems has always included people, process, and how they interact with systems. For the last few years, I have had opportunities to use that skillset in the manner of a UX Designer. And guess what? I love it! And since solving problems has always led to designing solutions in my world, a UX Designer emerged.

  • Maintains and promotes a futurist-thinking departmental focus.
  • Delivers design work on programs of any size with minimal oversight.
  • Effectively negotiates trade-offs with product management and engineering.
  • Executing design work consistent with design and brand standards, interaction patterns, analytics insight and customer feedback.
  • Reviews and interprets research findings and uses insights to refine and iterate on designs.
